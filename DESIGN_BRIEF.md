# Design Brief

## Style: Monochroma

The design should evoke a sense of elegance and refinement, with a minimalist layout that showcases a single color palette in a beautiful and sophisticated way. The overall atmosphere should be calm and serene, with a focus on the simplicity and beauty of the color itself. The interface should feature sleek, modern typography and graphics, with a focus on negative space and minimalism. Imagery should include stunning visuals of art, design, and fashion, with a focus on showcasing the nuances and subtleties of the color.

## Design Tokens

| Aspect | Direction |
|--------|-----------|
| Colors | Monochromatic, with a focus on showcasing the nuances and subtleties of a single color |
| Typography | Sleek and modern, with a focus on sans-serif fonts |
| Components | Sharp and angular buttons and cards, with a focus on minimalism and negative space |
| Imagery | High-quality images and illustrations of art, design, and fashion, with a focus on showcasing the beauty of the color |

## Theme Settings

- **Theme Color:** neutral
- **Mode:** light
- **Border Radius:** 0

## App Variant: ChromaPro

> Elevate Design With AI

ChromaPro is an advanced version of ChromaChroma, focusing on professional designers and artists. It incorporates AI-driven color theory to generate unique color palettes based on trending design styles and hashtags. This variant includes features like color palette export in various formats and integration with popular design tools.

**Target Audience:** Professional designers and artists
**Monetization:** Subscription-based model with a free trial period
**Complexity:** premium
